Four Cheeses Pasta

Description

This creamy dish is perfect for cold winter days.

Ingredients

(4 servings)
  • 400 g penne (or other pasta)
  • 1 yellow pepper
  • 100 g gorgonzola
  • 80 g parmigiano
  • 80 g taleggio
  • 80 g brie
  • 20 g butter
  • 4 spoons of cream
  • 1 glass of milk
  • Nutmeg
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Put on the heat a pot with water; when it boils, put the pasta in it.
  2. In the meantime, cut the yellow pepper in small pieces, and let some butter melt in a pan.
  3. Insert the pepper in the pan, cook for 2 minutes, then add the milk.
  4. Cut the gorgonzola into pieces and add it to the pan.
  5. Cut the taleggio and the brie into pieces and add them as well; if needed, add some milk.
  6. Add some nutmeg (if you like it) and let the cheeses melt on low heat, while the pasta cooks.
  7. When the pasta is almost done, add the cream and turn the heat to medium. Drain the pasta directly in the sauce.
  8. Off heat, add the parmigiano and stir the pasta well, so that it mixes with the sauce.
